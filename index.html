⚡ Residential Retail Energy

Looking for business energy? Visit our B2B Energy page →

HOW IT WORKS

In deregulated states, customers can choose who supplies their electricity or gas. Most don't know this. Our job is to educate them — clearly, honestly, compliantly.

Our trained agents operate in-store kiosks at Walmart, Sam's Club, Kroger, and other major retailers — meeting customers where they already shop. Supporting field teams extend reach into neighborhoods and events when the campaign calls for it.

WHY RETAIL?

Energy switching is a trust-based decision. Customers want a real person with real answers — and they're more receptive inside a store they already trust than on a cold doorstep.

Our reps educate — not pressure. The result is higher-quality customers with better retention for our partners.

🏢 Business-to-Business Energy

Commercial energy acquisition for businesses of every size. We walk into storefronts, offices, and industrial accounts — and we bring better rates with us.

Residential customer? Visit our Residential Energy page →

THE B2B OPPORTUNITY

Commercial accounts use more energy, sign longer-term contracts, and present bigger opportunities than residential customers. But they also require a different approach — a professional, consultative one.

Our B2B reps qualify accounts by usage, secure bill copies, present tailored savings, and close contracts with decision-makers. From single-location small business to multi-site enterprise, we move the needle.

WHY CIRCLE FOR B2B?

Commercial energy is won in face-to-face meetings, not cold emails. Business owners want a real person who understands their usage, their contract, and their bottom line.

Our commercial reps are trained on rate structures, contract terms, and objection handling specific to the B2B buyer. We walk in informed, and we walk out with signed agreements.
